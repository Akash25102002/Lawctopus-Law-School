Course Structure

Month-by-Month Breakdown

A structured 6-month journey — starting with fundamentals, deepening into specialist contracts, and ending with freelancing mastery.

M1–2
Months 1 & 2

Contract Fundamentals & Basic Negotiation

Elements of a valid contract, pre-contractual instruments, clause-by-clause drafting, orientation session, reviewing and redrafting a Sale Deed from scratch. Introduction to negotiation strategies you'll use in real life and transactions.

M3
Month 3

Common & International Agreements + Advanced Negotiation

NDAs, Loan Agreements, Employment Agreements, Service Agreements. Hands-on drafting with 7–8 live sessions. Advanced negotiation techniques applied to corporate deals and client consultations.

M4
Month 4

IP, Technology & Website Agreements

Trademark Licensing, SaaS Agreements, Privacy Policies, Terms & Conditions, Return & Refund Policies, e-contracts. Learn to serve the booming digital economy with specialist drafting skills.

M5
Month 5

Real Estate Agreements

Leave & License, Sale Deeds, Construction Contracts, Lease Agreements — one of the highest-paying areas of contract drafting in India.

M6
Month 6

Business Agreements & Freelancing Mastery

Shareholders Agreements, JVAs, Power of Attorney, Master Service Agreements. Plus: Upwork profile, proposals, direct client leads — with expert hand-holding.
